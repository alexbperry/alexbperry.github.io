Who I Am

Hey, I'm Alex I’m the kind of person who will build a website for fun, overthink a color palette, and then go spend a whole afternoon on Fortnite to reset my brain.


What I’m Into

  • Making things look good: I love playing with layouts, colors, and little details until something finally “clicks.” Websites, dashboards, scrapbooks, you name it.
  • Data & patterns: I will absolutely fall down a rabbit hole trying to turn messy information into something visual, simple, and satisfying.
  • Tech tinkering: Custom keyboards, random coding projects, dashboards, tiny tools that make life easier, or just building something because the idea won’t leave my brain.
  • Creative projects: Scrapbooks, surprise gifts, “just because” ideas, and anything that lets me mix design, photos, and little personal details.
  • Games & cozy chaos: Casual gaming sessions, silly ideas like cart ride games, and anything that feels a little unhinged but fun.

Work & Real-Life Me

When I’m not staring at a screen, I’ve spent summers up in a lifeguard stand watching over a pool, and I’ve done my time in the fast-paced world of taking orders and juggling a million small tasks at once. I like roles where I’m moving, problem-solving, and quietly keeping everything from falling apart.


How I Treat People

I’m big on thoughtfulness: handwritten notes, custom playlists, small details, and making people feel like I actually paid attention when they said they liked something once, six months ago.
