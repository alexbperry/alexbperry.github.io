What I’m Into

When I am not working on a project, I usually hop on Xbox for a bit. I mess around in the new Skate game, play a few Fortnite matches, or just zone out for a while. I am not a heavy gamer and it is really just how I reset. I also love the Triangle area. It has a big city feel without being overwhelming, and it is easily my favorite part of North Carolina.

Outside of that, I like doing fun and slightly chaotic things for no real reason. Casual gaming sessions, silly ideas, random side projects (making games in Roblox sucks!). I enjoy anything that feels light and creative.


Work & Real-Life Me

When I’m not behind a screen, I’m usually outside or moving around.
